Setting up Streak

To set up Streak, press the Save button on the settings pop-up. You will need to do this per card that you want a streak on.

Streak allows you to track habits that you don't do every day with the Days in week option. You can also customise whether your week starts on a Sunday or a Monday.

Your streak will be deleted if you delete the card that it is tracked on, or if you press the Delete button. This is an irreversible action.
